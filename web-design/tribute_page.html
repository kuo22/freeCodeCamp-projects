Stephen Robert Irwin

The great man who loved nature

Timeline of Steve Irwin

  • February 22, 1962 - Born in Essendon, Australia.
  • 1979 - Graduated from high school to become a crocodile hunter.
  • 1980s - Trapped and relocated rogue crocodiles to his family zoo.
  • 1991 - Became owner of the family business and started managing the wildlife park.
  • 1996 - The first episode of 'The Crocodile Hunter' was aired, which quickly became one of the international favorites.
  • 1998 - Steve continued successfully management the family owned wildlife parked. He renamed the park the ‘Australia Zoo’, and it was quickly gaining repute as one of the world’s best zoos.
  • 2002 - Steve founded the ‘Steve Irwin Conservation Foundation’, which was later renamed the ‘Wildlife Warriors’. The foundation was formed as an effort to educate people on the importance of protecting injured, threatened, and endangered wildlife.
  • 2006 - Passed away after being injured by a stingray.
